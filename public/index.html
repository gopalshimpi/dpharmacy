           Smt. Sharadchandrika Suresh Patil Institute of Technology,(D.Pharmacy) Chopda Dist. Jalgaon (M.S.) was established in 1982 under the guidance of Late Smt.Sharadchandrika Suresh Patil, Education minister of Maharashtra state and Hon.Dada saheb, Dr.Suresh G.Patil, Ex.M.L.A. and the founder president of M.G.S.Mandal Chopda. Since its inception, the institute has made remarkable progress in the field of Pharmacy education in rural area. The institute is one of the best Pharmacy institute in north Maharashtra. The Institute is recognized by AICTE, PCI & Govt. of Maharashtra. We have separate hostel facilities for boys and girls, well furnished Laboratories, Gymnasium, rich Library, Playground, Computer center, modern teaching facilities and above all highly qualified and experienced staff which cares for students as their wards.
          At present the Institute is making progress with leaps and bounds under unique guidance of Hon.Adv.Sandip Suresh Patil who is Chairman of Mahatma Gandhi Shikshan Mandal Chopda.
